## 📌 Executive Summary | الملخص التنفيذي

**DAY NIGHT DELIVERY SERVICES** is a UAE-based delivery, courier, transport, and logistics company providing fast, secure, and professional delivery solutions across the United Arab Emirates and selected international destinations.

**داي نايت لخدمات التوصيل والشحن** هي شركة إماراتية متخصصة في خدمات التوصيل والشحن والخدمات اللوجستية داخل دولة الإمارات وخارجها، وتقدم حلولاً احترافية للأفراد، المتاجر الإلكترونية، الشركات، المطاعم، الصيدليات، المؤسسات، والجهات الحكومية.

The company operates under a clear identity concept:

> **Day Night = Service by day and night, available 24/7, with speed, safety, and professional execution.**  
> **داي نايت = خدمة في النهار والليل، على مدار الساعة، بسرعة وأمان واحترافية.**

## 📦 Operational Workflow | نظام الاستلام والتسليم

### Required Order Data

- Sender name
- Sender phone
- Pickup address
- Pickup location
- Receiver name
- Receiver phone
- Delivery address
- Delivery location
- Shipment type
- Weight
- Number of pieces
- Service type
- Delivery fee
- Payment method
- Notes
- Order status

### Basic Workflow

```text
1. Customer contacts DAY NIGHT DELIVERY SERVICES
2. Order details are collected
3. Pickup location is confirmed
4. Delivery price is confirmed
5. Driver pickup is assigned
6. Shipment is collected
7. Tracking status is updated
8. Shipment is delivered
9. Proof of delivery is saved when required
10. Customer receives final confirmation
```
